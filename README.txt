Completed by Deli DeGrant

HOW TO PLAY
- Control the Rifle at the bottom left part of the screen using your mouse

- Left click to Fire a bullet

- Right click to Fire a strong bullet


Yellow filled circle are normal targets that can be terminated with just one bullets

Circle with numbers within are tough targets that are terminated with three bullets but once with a strong bullet 

Blue rectangles are safe targets. You loose points when hit!

Enjoy your game
